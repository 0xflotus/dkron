Job scheduling made easy, distributed and highly-available

Community Download

Dcron is a system service that runs scheduled tasks at given intervals or times, just like the cron unix service distributed in several machines in a cluster. If a machine fails (the leader), the next one will take the responsability to keep running the scheduled tasks. Without human intervention. Dcron is Open Source and freely available.
